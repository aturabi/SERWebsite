Aquaponics
Aquaculture, the art and science of fish farming, will also become an increasingly important method of food production in the coming century. Research has shown that people who consume fish in their regular diet have lower mortality rates from ischemic heart disease and stroke. Producing protein for human consumption is far more energy efficient than the production of other types of animal protein, reducing the climate impacts to fill these needs.

Aquaponics is the combination of hydroponics and aquaculture to create a contained miniature ecosystem. The fish waste is broken down by microorganisms into nutrients, which the plants then utilize for growth. When enacted with care, this system of production can also produce large amounts of vegetables and a moderate amount of fish mass.
