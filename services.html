  • Incubator - The SER Incubator offers farmland for lease to qualified program Experiencing to be a Farmer. Incubator sites are located in Mottas - Teresópolis, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Plots of land ranging from one-quarter to one acre, as well as greenhouse and cooler space, can be leased on an annual basis. The additional area may be available. The incubator is a very important component of our project will be the small farm incubator program in which we would provide space in Mottas, Teresopolis for small farmers, and families to learn hands on hydroponic farming technique and practical demonstrations of agricultural systems. These sessions will include technical information regarding the system design behind hydroponics, detail-oriented training, and community collaboration on greenhouse projects.
